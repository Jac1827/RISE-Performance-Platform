Training Objective

The training roadmap is built around Priority 1: fix lead intake and first-response reliability across both communities. The course is designed to improve speed-to-lead, discovery quality, follow-up discipline, and closing consistency while maintaining required response benchmarks and closing ratios.

Training Course Summary

  • Lead intake workflow and first-response reliability standards
  • Lead contact expectations across calls, texts, and email follow-up
  • Discovery questions, needs analysis, and next-step control
  • Tour execution, objection handling, and closing discipline
  • Follow-up quality review through call sentiment, coaching, and accountability

30 / 60 / 90 Day Roadmap

30 Days Complete in-person training at each community. Audit current lead intake and first-response practices, reinforce discovery and tour execution, and standardize follow-up expectations to support stronger closing consistency.
31-60 Days Conduct May in-person follow-up visits with Morgan Matlock. Review response times, lead contact completion, tour conversion, and closing performance; coach teams on gaps immediately when benchmarks are not being met.
61-90 Days Continue virtual follow-up calls and corporate support. Morgan will monitor follow-up execution and lead contacts, review call sentiments, and provide weekly coaching whenever required benchmarks or closing ratios fall below expectations.

Ongoing Accountability

  • Morgan Matlock will monitor lead follow-up activity and contact cadence to ensure timely responses.

  • Weekly coaching will be provided when teams are not meeting required benchmarks.
